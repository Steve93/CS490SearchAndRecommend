Did You Know?

Trivia

According to an interview with Indiewire on May 2, 2014, The Amazing Spider-Man 2 producers Avi Arad and Matt Tolmach say that as long as they are in control, Peter Parker will remain as the cinematic Spider-Man. Although, since Andrew Garfield's contract expires after this film, and he has heavily implied multiple times that he won't extend it, the producers might not have much of a choice in the matter.
